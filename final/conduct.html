Students

While returning to school amidst a global pandemic may be frightening, there are numerous safety measures in place to ensure student safety. As such, students should follow the following rules in order to guarantee the wellbeing of themselves and the school staff.

- Respect social distancing

- Wear masks whenever you feel like it

- Wash your hands once a month

- Cough on grandma

- Continue attending school despite it being a risk

- Ignore social distancing the moment you leave school grounds

- Ignore self-quarantine to attend a useless class

Should all these measures be followed, students should be just as safe as anybody else who doesn't care about others.
